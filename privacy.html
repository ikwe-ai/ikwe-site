Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Visible Healing Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use ikwe.ai and our related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Email Address

When you subscribe to our newsletter, request an audit, or contact us through our forms, we collect your email address. This information is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send you updates about our research and services
  • Provide access to benchmark results and resources

Form Submissions

When you submit a form through our website (via Jotform), we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Company or organization name
  • Information about your AI system or evaluation needs

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect sensitive demo input. Our public demos are accessible without requiring personal information, and we do not store or analyze the content of conversations you have with our demo tools.

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies beyond what is necessary for basic website functionality.

How We Store Your Information

Email addresses and form submissions are stored securely through our third-party service provider (Jotform). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Provide evaluation and benchmarking services
  • Send you updates about our research, benchmark results, and new tools
  • Improve our services and website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use Jotform to collect and manage form submissions. Jotform's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
