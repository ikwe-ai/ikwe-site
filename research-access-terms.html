Research Access Terms

Effective date: February 12, 2026. Owner: Visible Healing Inc. (dba Ikwe.ai). These terms govern access to Ikwe research artifacts, methodology documents, and gated materials.

Scope: Access supports institutional review, peer validation, and diligence. Access does not transfer ownership, implementation rights, or license rights.

1. Ownership

All research materials, frameworks, methodologies, scoring systems, model taxonomies, benchmark outputs, and related derivatives are owned by Visible Healing Inc. (dba Ikwe.ai). All rights are reserved unless explicitly granted in a separate written agreement.

2. Permitted Use

Approved use includes academic or professional review, methodological critique, peer validation, internal institutional evaluation, and quotation of short excerpts with attribution.

3. Prohibited Use

  • Reverse engineering proprietary signal architecture, weighting logic, or intervention thresholds.
  • Derivative implementation of Ikwe methods without written permission.
  • Redistribution of gated materials.
  • Commercial use of restricted artifacts without a signed agreement.

4. Tiered Disclosure

Research materials are disclosed in tiers: Public, Validated (gated), Partner (NDA required), and Internal (restricted). Each tier carries specific access limits.

5. Citation and Attribution

Citations must follow the Ikwe Citation Guide. Citation permission does not authorize republication of full gated documents.

6. No Clinical, Legal, or Medical Advice

Research materials are informational and research-oriented. They are not legal advice, medical advice, clinical guidance, or emergency response services.

7. Access Controls and Enforcement

Ikwe may log artifact access, revoke access for misuse, and pursue legal remedies for IP misuse, unauthorized redistribution, or unauthorized derivative implementation.

8. Contact

For research-access questions: research@ikwe.ai
For legal inquiries: legal@ikwe.ai
