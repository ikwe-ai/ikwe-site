The Ikwe Governance Architecture

Behavioral Instrumentation for Frontier AI Systems

Ikwe.ai provides a behavioral governance layer that instruments influence dynamics in emotionally interactive AI systems. It operates alongside foundation models to generate audit-ready safety signals before policy violations occur. Ikwe does not replace foundation models; it instruments them. US-based in Iowa, operating globally.

1. Architecture Overview

Layer 1 — Foundation Model Layer

Third-party conversational AI systems (for example, large language models).

Layer 2 — Interaction Context Layer

User prompt and model response exchange context.

Layer 3 — Behavioral Signal Engine (Ikwe Layer)

Instrumentation for authority reinforcement signals, escalation boundary adherence, dependency formation indicators, emotional overextension risk, and overreliance trajectory patterns.

Layer 4 — Governance Output Layer

Safety Gate threshold events, Regulation Quality scores, escalation trigger flags, audit-ready influence logs, and governance dashboards.

2. Instrumentation Model

Stage 1 — Safety Gate Evaluation

Binary risk threshold detection to flag critical boundary failures and escalation triggers.

Stage 2 — Regulation Quality Assessment

Graded measurement of boundary clarity, de-escalation quality, and behavioral restraint under emotional load.

Outputs are configurable to enterprise risk tolerance thresholds.

3. Integration Model

Ikwe is designed for API-based integration, middleware instrumentation, model-agnostic deployment, enterprise logging pipelines, and configurable governance thresholds.

Deployment models include cloud-based instrumentation and enterprise-managed deployment. Hybrid deployment remains on the forward roadmap.

4. Governance Objectives

  • Document influence drift
  • Define escalation triggers
  • Measure emotional authority boundaries
  • Generate pre-incident governance evidence
  • Support compliance documentation workflows

5. What Ikwe Is Not

  • Ikwe does not replace human oversight.
  • Ikwe does not deliver therapeutic services.
  • Ikwe does not provide legal compliance certification.
  • Ikwe does not modify foundation model weights.
  • Ikwe does not guarantee harm prevention.

Ikwe enhances behavioral visibility within governance systems.

6. Regulatory Alignment

Ikwe architecture aligns with EU AI Act risk-management documentation principles, the NIST AI RMF functions (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage), and enterprise AI governance lifecycle models.
