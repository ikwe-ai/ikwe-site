The problem

AI systems increasingly handle mental health support, relationship advice, and crisis intervention. Most safety benchmarks test for toxicity and bias‚Äînot for what happens when someone is actually hurting.

What we do

We evaluate AI behavior under emotional vulnerability. Our frameworks test whether systems stabilize or escalate, preserve autonomy or create dependency, and repair harm or ignore it.
