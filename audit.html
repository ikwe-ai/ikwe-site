Risk & Harm Pattern Audit

A scoped, paid engagement that shows where AI risk is forming early — and where teams can still intervene.

What this audit does

Most AI safety reviews look at isolated outputs or post-incident failures. This audit looks at how risk forms over time, across real interaction trajectories.

The goal is not to score your system. The goal is to give you time — earlier visibility so decisions can change outcomes.

Deliverables

Risk Pattern Map

A visual map showing where harm and escalation begin across interaction trajectories.

Early Signal Indicators

The specific signals that appear before incidents and are missed by standard safety checks.

Intervention Points

Clear points where teams can act earlier — product, policy, monitoring, or deployment changes.

Executive Readout

A working session translating findings into decisions your team can make immediately.

How it works

Scope (1–2 weeks)

Analysis

Readout
