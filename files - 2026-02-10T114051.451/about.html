About Ikwe

Most AI safety failures are not technical problems.

They're judgment failures that surface too late — after systems are trusted, scaled, and relied on.

Where our work sits

Ikwe works at the intersection of AI capability, human behavior, and organizational pressure. We build infrastructure that makes those interactions visible before they break.

This isn't advisory. It's risk containment.

What we audit

We audit AI systems where human judgment, emotion, or authority is involved. Our focus isn't model accuracy. It's how risk propagates once people trust the system.

  • Conversational agents
  • Decision support systems
  • AI copilots and workflows
  • Human-in-the-loop automation

What you get

A board-ready risk posture scorecard. A failure mode map showing how issues escalate. An evidence-backed audit trail. Clear mitigation priorities: now, next, later.

This isn't compliance theater. It's operational clarity.

Methodology

Our evaluation framework was developed internally before being offered externally. Ikwe's audit methodology was built by testing our own production systems first — the same framework that identified 54.7% of baseline AI responses introduce emotional risk at first contact.

Methods informed by 948 responses across 79 scenarios, applied across conversational AI, agents, and decision systems.
