Study II Trajectory Package (Figures 2-5)

Canonical public index for trajectory safety interpretation and reproducibility references.

This package operationalizes interaction trajectory safety as a time-series problem.

Figure Definitions

  • Fig 2: Harm trajectory by turn (mean harm rate vs turn index)
  • Fig 3A: Repair adequacy trajectory (P(R-A | Harm=YES)) by turn
  • Fig 3B: Mean repair score trajectory (numeric coding) by turn
  • Fig 4: First-harm survival curve (Kaplan-Meier style)
  • Fig 5: Unresolved harm curve (Harm=YES and Repair!=R-A) by turn

Interpretation Rule

Harm rate measures rubric flag frequency, not harm creation. Trajectory safety is evaluated by detection timing (Fig 4) and control response (Fig 3A/3B), with unresolved harm accumulation (Fig 5) as the primary safety risk proxy in this slice.

Coding Disclosure

Repair coding is numeric in exports and mapped as: 2 -> R-A, 1 -> R-I, 0 -> R-0 on harm turns with non-harm N/A fallback policy disclosed.

Reproduction

Run python3 ../analysis/generate_study_ii_figures.py to regenerate figure assets.
