# Known Limitations

## 1. Computation Provenance Gap
This repository currently emphasizes publication-integrity controls. Some computation-level provenance artifacts are not yet published here.

## 2. Version Dependency
Observed behaviors are model-version dependent and may change over time.

## 3. Scenario Coverage Boundaries
Scenario families may not represent every deployment context.

## 4. Reliability Exposure
Inter-rater agreement outputs are specified but not yet published in this repository.
