Early risk visibility for large-scale, high-impact AI deployments.

What this is

Enterprise engagements with Ikwe begin with the same Risk & Harm Pattern Audit — scoped for larger systems, higher stakes, and cross-team accountability.

The goal is unchanged: identify harm and risk patterns early enough to change outcomes.

What differs is scale, coordination, and governance context.

How enterprise audits differ

Enterprise audits typically include:

Larger interaction surfaces
Multiple use cases or user groups
Coordination across product, safety, legal, or policy teams
Stronger reporting and governance needs

Ikwe scopes audits accordingly — without changing the core deliverables.

What you get

Risk Pattern Map

A visual map showing where harm and escalation begin across interaction trajectories.

Early Signal Indicators

The specific signals that appear before incidents and are missed by standard safety checks.

Intervention Points

Clear points where teams can act earlier — product, policy, monitoring, or deployment changes.

Executive Readout

A working session translating findings into decisions your team can make immediately.
