About Visible Healing Inc.

The company behind ikwe.ai

Visible Healing Inc. is an AI safety evaluation company based in Des Moines, Iowa. We build tools and frameworks to measure what others miss: how AI systems behave when humans are vulnerable.

Our flagship product, the EQ Safety Benchmark, evaluates behavioral safety in emotionally responsive AI—testing not whether systems can recognize emotions, but whether they respond safely under real-world vulnerability.

What We Do

Emotional AI Safety Evaluation

We test AI systems against scenarios involving distress, crisis, interpersonal conflict, and vulnerable states. Our evaluation measures regulation timing, repair behavior, and safety gate violations.

Benchmark Development

The EQ Safety Benchmark is an evolving framework for measuring behavioral safety. It currently includes 74 scenarios across 14 emotional categories, with ongoing expansion.

Advisory & Research

We work with AI teams building products that touch human vulnerability—mental health apps, companion systems, customer support AI, and educational platforms.

Our Approach

We believe emotional safety in AI requires more than good intentions. It requires:

Measurement over assumption

If you can't measure safety behavior, you can't improve it.

Behavior over recognition

Detecting emotion is not the same as responding safely.

Restraint over engagement

Sometimes the safest response is knowing when not to respond.

Repair over perfection

Errors are inevitable. What matters is what happens next.
