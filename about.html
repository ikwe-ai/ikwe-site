About Ikwe

Most AI safety failures are not technical problems.

They're judgment failures that surface too late — after systems are trusted, scaled, and relied on.

Where Our Work Sits

Between AI capability, human behavior, and organizational pressure.

We build infrastructure that makes those interactions visible before they break. This isn't advisory. It's risk containment.

01

AI Capability

Systems are becoming more capable faster than governance can adapt. Capability without oversight creates compounding risk.

02

Human Behavior

Users trust AI systems in ways designers don't anticipate. Emotional reliance, authority acceptance, and dependency form silently.

03

Organizational Pressure

Speed-to-market incentives override safety review. Governance becomes reactive instead of structural. Risk is accepted by default.
