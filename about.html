About ikwe.ai

Independent research on behavioral emotional safety.

What We Do

We measure how AI responses introduce and manage emotional risk — and whether that risk is repaired within the interaction.

Most benchmarks assume harm comes from users. We measure what the AI response itself introduces when vulnerability is present.

Core Finding

Recognition ≠ Safety

An AI can name emotion, sound empathetic, and still behave unsafely. Models with highest emotional fluency performed worst on behavioral safety under distress.

Organization

ikwe.ai is a research initiative of Visible Healing Inc., an Iowa-based AI research company focused on emotional safety infrastructure.

We operate independently, without funding from the AI companies we evaluate.

Work With Us

We partner with teams building emotionally-aware AI, investors evaluating AI safety, and policymakers shaping regulation.
