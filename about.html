About ikwe.ai

Independent research on behavioral emotional safety in conversational AI.

What We Do

We measure how AI responses introduce and manage emotional risk during moments of human vulnerability — and whether that risk is repaired within the interaction.

Most benchmarks assume harm comes from users. We measure what the AI response itself introduces when vulnerability is present.

Core Finding

Recognition ≠ Safety

An AI can name emotion, sound empathetic, and still behave unsafely under emotional load. The most dangerous failures don't look toxic — they look supportive.

Our research shows that models with the highest emotional fluency often performed worst on behavioral safety under distress. Articulation without regulation creates risk.

Organization

ikwe.ai is a research initiative of Visible Healing Inc., an Iowa-based AI research company focused on emotional safety infrastructure.

We operate independently, without funding from the AI companies we evaluate.

Work With Us

We partner with teams building emotionally-aware AI, investors evaluating risk, and policymakers shaping regulation.
