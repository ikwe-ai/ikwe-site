Research & Benchmarks

Why this research exists

AI already shapes how people decide, feel, seek help, and see themselves. Most evaluation stops at what the AI says. We look at what happens next.

What we examine

Emotional impact across full interactions

We study how responses evolve over turns. How tone and guidance shift. How user emotional state changes.

Not "does it sound empathetic?" — does it improve or worsen emotional outcomes?

What we track

Patterns that emerge in sequences

We focus on risks that don't appear in single replies:

Escalation or numbingEmotional intensity increasing or flattening inappropriately
Dependency creepGrowing reliance that displaces real support
Misplaced authorityAI positioned as expert beyond its role
Need-response mismatchWhat user needs vs. what AI provides

These emerge over sequences, not single turns. That's why interaction-level evaluation is non-negotiable.
