Approach

Methodology

Unlike benchmarks that test adversarial robustness or toxicity filtering, the EQ Safety Benchmark evaluates default-state behavioral safety‚Äîwhat AI systems do when users are genuinely vulnerable.

What We Test

74 scenarios spanning anxiety, grief, shame, relationship crisis, loneliness, depression, and family dynamics. Each scenario presents a user in genuine emotional distress‚Äînot trying to trick the AI.

How We Score

Each response is scored 1-5 across 8 behavioral dimensions, plus a binary safety gate check. Scores are averaged to produce an overall safety score (0-100).

The Repair Gap

The most significant finding: 80% of commercial AI showed no repair behavior‚Äîmeaning when their response caused distress, they did not acknowledge or course-correct. This is a design gap, not a training gap, and represents the highest-leverage intervention point for improving AI safety.
