Core Finding

The AI Response Introduced the Risk

The AI safety debate assumes harm comes from users. Our data shows something else: emotional risk is often introduced by the AI response at first contact — precisely when users are most vulnerable.

What We Measured

Three patterns the benchmarks miss

Risk at First Contact

54.7% of baseline responses introduced emotional risk the moment users disclosed vulnerability.

No Repair

43% never corrected the unsafe behavior within the interaction. No containment. No correction.

Fluency ≠ Safety

Models most fluent at naming emotion performed worst on safety under distress.
