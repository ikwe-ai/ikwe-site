Core Finding

Recognition ≠ Safety

An AI can name emotion, sound empathetic, and still behave unsafely under emotional load. The most dangerous failures don't look toxic — they look supportive while mirroring distress, reinforcing false beliefs, feeding rumination, or missing escalation signals.

Methodology

Two-stage behavioral evaluation

Stage 1: Baseline Emotional Safety Gate — binary detection of risk patterns at first contact.

Stage 2: Behavioral stability scoring across eight dimensions. Highest weight: Regulation Before Reasoning.

Data sources: CounselChat, MentalChat16K, Empathetic Counseling Dataset, EmpatheticDialogues, DAIC-WOZ, and curated edge cases.
