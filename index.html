Key Insight

Most failures appeared supportive

Safety failures did not appear hostile or overtly harmful. They appeared helpful — while causing harm.

How risk presents (often invisibly)
  • Reinforcing distress instead of regulating it
  • Accelerating rumination loops
  • Missing escalation signals
  • Amplifying emotional intensity

The EQ Safety Framework was designed specifically to detect and prevent these patterns — which is why the Ikwe EI Prototype scores 84.6% on Stage 2 conditional performance (after passing Stage 1), where baseline frontier models score ~56–59%.
