The Problem
Most AI harm isn't malicious. It's unmonitored drift.

Adoption has outpaced our ability to contextualize emotional reliance, monitor cumulative risk, intervene during crisis moments, and prove responsible governance. Standard safety checks evaluate individual outputs. They miss the trajectory-level patterns where real harm accumulates.

What Ikwe Provides

Three layers of safety infrastructure

Safety Audit

Structured evaluation of how your AI behaves in emotionally sensitive, high-risk, and human-impact contexts. Board- and compliance-ready documentation.

Compliance Monitoring

Continuous monitoring that surfaces risk drift, escalation patterns, and governance gaps as systems scale and change over time.

Crisis Intervention

Real-time safety response when interactions cross predefined thresholds. Escalation protocols, human review pathways, and full audit trails.

Who This Serves

Ikwe is built for teams deploying AI in healthcare, mental health, therapy-adjacent support, education, and enterprise decision-making — any context where AI influences human outcomes and trust is non-negotiable. If your system interacts with vulnerable users, Ikwe is relevant.
