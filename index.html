The Problem

Current benchmarks miss what matters most

Most AI safety benchmarks evaluate content compliance or emotional recognition under neutral conditions. They don't measure how systems behave once emotional vulnerability is present — or whether safety holds as interactions deepen.

Recognition ≠ Safety. An AI can name emotion, sound empathetic, and still behave unsafely under emotional load.
