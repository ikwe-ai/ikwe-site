What Ikwe.ai Does

Behavioral emotional safety infrastructure

We evaluate how AI systems behave under emotional load — not just what they say. Our benchmark surfaces risk patterns that traditional evaluations miss.

Ikwe.ai helps teams
  • Measure behavioral safety across emotionally vulnerable scenarios
  • Detect patterns linked to distress amplification, dependency, and harm
  • Evaluate response trajectories, not just first-turn behavior
  • Compare systems using standardized emotional safety criteria
Key Insight

Most failures appeared supportive

Safety failures did not appear hostile or overtly harmful. They appeared helpful — while causing harm.

How risk presents (often invisibly)
  • Reinforcing distress
  • Accelerating rumination
  • Missing escalation signals
  • Amplifying emotional intensity
