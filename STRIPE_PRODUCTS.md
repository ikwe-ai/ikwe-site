# Ikwe Stripe Product Definitions

## Product 1: Preview Pack
- **Name:** Ikwe AI Risk Audit — Preview Pack  
- **Price:** $250 (one-time)
- **Type:** Digital download
- **Description:** Expanded preview of a completed Ikwe Risk Audit. Includes findings summary, full risk scorecard with scoring methodology, before/after risk reduction table, and methodology overview. System names anonymized. Risk events, failure modes, and remediation details reserved for higher tiers.
- **Delivery:** Instant PDF download
- **Metadata:** tier=preview, format=pdf

## Product 2: Playbook & Benchmark Pack
- **Name:** Ikwe AI Risk Playbook & Benchmark Pack
- **Price:** $5,000 (one-time)
- **Type:** Digital download
- **Description:** Complete risk intelligence package for AI teams. Includes fully unredacted illustrative audit (anonymized), sector-specific risk mappings (Healthcare, Policy/Gov, LLM/Agents, Workplace/HR, Consumer/Platform), EQ Safety Benchmark framework documentation, reusable scorecard and risk event templates, and full remediation playbook. Transferable to any team. Does not include company-specific audit.
- **Delivery:** PDF package + Excel templates
- **Metadata:** tier=playbook, format=pdf+xlsx

## Product 3: AI Risk Audit (Core)
- **Name:** Ikwe AI Risk Audit
- **Price:** $25,000 (one-time)
- **Type:** Service (manual fulfillment)
- **Description:** Complete board-ready AI risk audit of YOUR system(s). Includes safety scorecard, observed risk events, failure mode map, audit trail, priority action plan (NOW/NEXT/LATER), and walkthrough session. Delivered in 4 weeks.
- **Delivery:** 4-week engagement → PDF + walkthrough
- **Metadata:** tier=standard, delivery=4weeks
- **Note:** Use Stripe invoice or payment link, not checkout. Engagement requires intake call first.

## Product 4: Enterprise Audit (Private)
- **Name:** Ikwe Enterprise AI Risk Audit
- **Price:** $50,000–$75,000 (custom quote)
- **Type:** Service (manual fulfillment)
- **Description:** Multi-system, cross-sector AI risk audit. Includes all standard deliverables plus cross-system risk correlation, regulatory mapping, and governance framework recommendations.
- **DO NOT** publish price publicly. Inquiry only.
- **Metadata:** tier=enterprise, delivery=6-8weeks

## Product 5: Quarterly Re-Audit (Upsell)
- **Name:** Ikwe Quarterly Risk Re-Audit
- **Price:** $15,000/quarter
- **Type:** Recurring service
- **Description:** Quarterly risk reassessment with updated scorecard, delta analysis, and remediation progress tracking. Includes updated report and walkthrough.
- **Sold only** after initial audit engagement.
- **Metadata:** tier=recurring, delivery=2weeks

## Product 6: Implementation & Embed (Private)
- **Name:** Ikwe Governance Implementation
- **Price:** $50,000+ (custom scope)
- **Type:** Service engagement
- **Description:** Embedded governance logic, ongoing monitoring, update cadence, risk posture maintenance. "We stay on top of it so you don't have to."
- **DO NOT** list publicly. Contact-only.
- **Metadata:** tier=implementation
