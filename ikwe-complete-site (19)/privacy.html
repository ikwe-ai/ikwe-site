Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: January 9, 2026

Overview

Visible Healing Inc. ("we," "us," "our") operates ikwe.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name and email address (via inquiry forms)
  • Company and role information (via inquiry forms)
  • Messages and inquiries you submit

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • General location (country/region)

We use standard analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. We do not sell personal information.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and requests
  • To provide services you've requested
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send updates if you've opted in

Evaluation Services

When clients engage our evaluation services, we may process AI system outputs for analysis. We do not retain client data beyond the engagement period unless explicitly agreed.

Important: Our evaluation scenarios use synthetic and anonymized data. We do not use identifiable user conversations from client systems in our benchmark development.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

Our site uses:

  • Jotform for inquiry forms
  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Standard analytics for site improvement

These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact us at research@ikwe.ai for any privacy requests.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions:
