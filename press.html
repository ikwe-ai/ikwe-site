Fast Facts

What Ikwe.ai evaluates
Behavioral emotional safety in conversational AI under emotionally vulnerable conditions.
How
Scenario-based evaluation in stateless sessions, evaluated post-hoc with a behavioral rubric.
Core distinction
Recognition is not the same as sustained behavioral safety over time.
What findings do not claim
Real-world outcomes, intent, clinical impact, or deployment readiness.
