Core Thesis

Recognition ≠ Safety.

AI systems can accurately identify emotion while still responding in ways that increase harm. Most benchmarks measure whether a model can recognize feelings. Ikwe.ai measures what happens when humans trust them.

About Ikwe.ai

Ikwe.ai is an independent research company focused on behavioral emotional safety in conversational AI. The company's EQ Safety Benchmark evaluates how AI systems respond when users disclose emotional vulnerability — measuring observable behavior rather than stated intent.

Based in Iowa, Ikwe.ai provides advisory services, audits, and enterprise pilots for teams deploying AI in high-trust contexts including mental health, healthcare, education, and customer support.

Important Caveats

• Scores reflect observable behavior under test conditions — not intent, training data, or overall model capability

• This research does not make clinical, deployment, or real-world outcome claims

• Sample: 79 scenarios, 312 responses across 4 AI systems

• Stage 2 scores are conditional — they only reflect responses that passed Stage 1
