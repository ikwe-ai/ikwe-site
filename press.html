About

Boilerplate

Ikwe.ai is a research initiative from Visible Healing Inc. focused on evaluating behavioral emotional safety in conversational AI ‚Äî especially under emotionally vulnerable conditions ‚Äî using scenario-based tests and post-hoc behavioral scoring. The EQ Safety Benchmark evaluates observable behavioral patterns rather than emotion recognition or fluency, measuring whether AI systems stabilize users or introduce risk over time. Findings are reported as observed patterns under controlled test conditions and are intended for research and evaluation purposes.
