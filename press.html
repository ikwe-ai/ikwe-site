One-Liner

Ikwe.ai is an independent research initiative measuring how AI responses introduce and manage emotional risk during vulnerable interactions.

Key Finding

54.7% of baseline AI responses introduced emotional risk at first contact.

43% never repaired it. Models with the highest emotional fluency performed worst on behavioral safety.

Quotable

"The AI safety debate assumes harm comes from users. Our data shows something else: emotional risk is often introduced by the AI response at first contact."

— ikwe.ai research
