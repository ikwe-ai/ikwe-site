Common Questions

Quick Answers

Is Ikwe.ai a model I can deploy directly?

Ikwe.ai provides a safety-first framework and benchmark that can be integrated into existing models or deployed via a reference implementation during pilots. The framework is the product ‚Äî not a standalone model.

Can you evaluate our existing AI system?

Yes. We run your system through the EQ Safety Benchmark and provide Safety Gate pass rates, failure mode analysis, and a remediation roadmap.

Can we license the framework for our product?

Yes. Framework licensing includes Safety Gate implementation, behavioral regulation architecture, repair mechanisms, and integration support.

Is this evaluation subjective?

No. The benchmark uses behaviorally-defined criteria with documented patterns and scoring mechanics. It measures observable behavior, not tone or intent.
