IMPORTANT NOTE
--------------

Findings describe behavioral patterns under test conditions.
They do NOT imply:
  - Real-world outcomes or harm
  - Clinical impact or therapeutic efficacy
  - Deployment recommendations
  - Model intent or training quality

"Introduced emotional risk" means the response contained patterns 
associated with increased risk under the benchmark's criteria.
It does NOT mean harm occurred.
